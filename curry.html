Thai Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

This recipe is pretty simple and does not need a lot of different ingredients. You can buy everything in a single trip to the supermarket, and of course, you can substitute a lot of the ingredients as you see fit!.

Here are all the things you need:

  1. 4 chicken breasts
  2. 200gr of green peas
  3. 1 can of coconut milk
  4. Spices
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Chilli powder
    • Tumeric
  5. 500gr white rice

Instructions

First, cut your chicken in smaller pieces. Aim for small unifrom cubes. This will add a lot of visual pleasentness to your food. Chop your onions and put them on low heat and stir them until they are golden brown.

When the onions are ready, add your chicken. Stir them together until your chicken is not pink on the outside. Now, add your spices and let them "bloom" for a while. This is an importnat step, as blooming your spices adds a lot of taste to your dish.

Now you can add your green peas and the coconut milk to the mix. You can now put the lid on and let your curry cook on low hit for a while. Remember:The curry has to come to boil first, then you can turn the heat low and let it simmer.

As you are waiting for your curry to finish cooking, you should start cooking your rice in a small pot. This should take somewhere around 15 minutes. As always, make sure your are adding just the right amount of water to your rice - not too much, not too little!

That's it! Enjoy your curry.
